About venuefinder.com

Established in 1978, venuefinder.com / blue&green has evolved into the most comprehensive and reliable directory and website for event organisers booking venues.

It is the only ABC audited venue directory with 39,770 unique users and 50,537 visits per month.

Users of venuefinder.com / blue&green include event organisers from agencies, associations, pharmaceutical, financial, and manufacturing organisations.

Rather than making hundreds of phone calls, we provide you with a comprehensive venue listing right here on our site.

Just enter your important details, such as the potential number of delegates attending and the number of rooms required, and we will list a variety of locations suitable to your individual needs.

Anyone using venuefinder.com will be linked up to over 15,000 event spaces in the UK and internationally. So, for wedding venues in Kent or a meeting room in Italy, we can help out regardless of the type or location.

How do I search for a venue?

Use the venue search button to search for a venue by country, venue name, town, county, capacity, catering capacity, number of meeting rooms and venue bedrooms. For example, if you are looking for venues in London, enter "London" into the town search box and click search. The search will return any matching venues in that area. Follow the links to view each venue.

How do I get a listing on venuefinder.com ?

If you would like a free listing on venuefinder.com click on advertiser zone from the green horizontal bar at the top of the main venuefinder.com website and choose one of the 4 options within the new entries box. Fill in the online form and click the submit button to send your details direct to the venuefinder.com / blue&green team. Your will then receive an email back from us with your unique user name and password allowing you to complete your entry on venuefinder.com . Your details will then be validated and uploaded to venuefinder.com .

Tell me more about venuefinder.com Express Meetings

How does the Express Meetings service work?
The venuefinding service is free service to you, as an agency we are paid a commission by your chosen venue once your event is contracted and has taken place

Do I have to choose the venue?
We would request that you pick a venue for us to approach, however if you do not know of any then we will find the perfect venue for your requirements and budget

How long will the process take?
We aim to have provided you with a full proposal of a venue with costs within 2 hours of receiving the enquiry

What happens if I don't like the venue offered or location?
Our team will contact you when the proposal is ready and talk you through the venue. Should you decide you're not happy with the venue then your enquiry will be moved to the normal venue finding process and we will then provide 3 suitable venues within a timeline agreed between both parties

Are there any hidden costs?
There are no costs incurred by you for the venue finding service, once the venue is confirmed the venue will require payment details from you for your conference booking, all we ask if that the booking is fair and honest.

Will I lose my contact with the hotel?
Not at all, we will purely do the initial booking and confirm the space. Once the booking is confirmed the venue will be in contact with you to arrange the final details and contract for your event

Can you help with the contracts?
Yes we can help with checking the contracts and making sure that the terms and conditions are all in line with your event. We are not legal experts, however we do deal with many events and understand event contracts and cancellation charges.

Venuefinder.com Express Meetings Terms and Conditions apply:

* You must have an exact date required for your meeting
* The enquiry must be genuine and be taking place (e.g. not a price comparison exercise)
* Should you not be happy with the venue or your requirements change your booking will be transferred to the normal venue finding process and a date will be agreed to have 3 venues proposed.
* There are no minimum or maximum numbers for your delegates
* This is a free venue finding service and no cost will be incurred by you.
